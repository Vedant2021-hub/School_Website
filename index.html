Library

The Global Indian International School Library consists of different books to cater for our school population. In the library we believe that the development of literacy skills and the acquisition of knowledge are paramount to progress in education. We encourage students, parents, teachers and non-academic staff to inform the library staff on regular basis of what they would like to see in the library. This leads to a balanced acquisition programme that ensures both academic and recreational needs are catered for.

Inter School Football Championship
Came second in football inter school championship

Global Indian International School Noida proudly shares the victory of its Football team at the Inter-School Football Fest held at Basant Kunj, New Delhi. Our team of 9 players brought the winner’s trophy home after competing with 6 different schools from DelhiNcr. The first match was played between Millennium School, Gurgaon and GIIS Noida. Both the teams played exceptionally well which lead to a tie breaker round. Penalty shootout (which happens when there’s a tie) of 5 chances was given to each team, which was won by GIIS Noida. After qualifying for the second round, GIIS Noida played and won another match against Son Valley School Delhi. After winning both the matches, our soccer enthusiasts also won the final match by scoring 4 goals against 1 by Millennium School, Noida.
